Sponsorship

Partner with NAAMA to put Arab American health front and center at the 48th National Medical Convention — September 4–6, 2026, Houston, Texas.

Become a Partner

The National Arab American Medical Association is a leading non-profit organization advancing the medical and educational interests of Arab American healthcare professionals — a network of more than 6,000 physicians and over 50 NAAMA NextGen chapters nationwide. The 48th National Medical Convention, held in collaboration with Baylor College of Medicine, brings together physicians, researchers, healthcare leaders, and trainees from across the country. Your sponsorship places your organization at the center of that conversation.

How to Become a Sponsor

1
Choose Your Level
Select a sponsorship level — Diamond, Sapphire, Gold, or Silver — or an advertising package that fits your goals.
2

Pay by check (payable to NAAMA), credit card, or bank wire. Email naama@naama.com for wire instructions.

Submit the signed Written Agreement for Commercial Support along with your logo and advertising materials.
