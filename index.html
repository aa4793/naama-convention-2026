About the Convention
Arab American Health, Front and Center

The National Arab American Medical Association's 48th annual convention convenes clinicians, trainees, researchers, and community leaders to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and advance health equity. This year's program focuses on Arab American health across clinical care, public health, research, policy, and community engagement — over Labor Day weekend in Houston, Texas.
